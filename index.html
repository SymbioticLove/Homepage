Who we are

Symbiotic Love™ means community. Follow us on social media, join the discussion.

We are a small start-up that hopes to one day use the voice of the community we build to create real, tangible change for global issues. We are entirely self-funded. Our talented designer, Jessela, creates all of our assets by hand (hence the placeholders!). We are working tirelessly to give you the best experience possible. We are not asking for your money, we are asking for your time. Help us spread awareness of these issues. Help us support change.

How you can help

Reach out and connect with us by clicking on Feedback@symbiotic.love to let us know what you like, what you don't like and what you'd like to see changed or added! If you want to add something yourself, you can visit the Github repository for Symbiotic.love by clicking on the logo (currently a moon) at the top of the screen. Anyone may propose changes.
