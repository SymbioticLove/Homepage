We are proud to announce our support for the Effective Altruism Foundation!

We are proud to announce that for the month of May, we will be contributing 10% of our sales (as well as 100% of any event donations or contributions) to the Effective Altruism Foundation. We will be posting our MSF donation numbers here when the month is concluded. The primary purpose of the EA Foundation, as stated on their website, is "...to undertake and contribute to research on how to improve the quality of the lives of as many sentient beings as extensively as possible". This mission is aligned perfectly with our own, and as such, we are happy to contribute to their efforts. If you have the time, please visit https://ea-foundation.org/eaf-usa/ to read and learn more.
