Compatibility Instructions

  1. Right click anywhere on the web page (the main page, which should still be open)
  2. Select "Inspect", usually located near the bottom of the list
  3. In the menu now open on the right-hand side, at the top, select the icon that looks like a tablet and a phone. It will read "Toggle device emulation" if you hover over it.
  4. In this viewing mode, a new menu will appear at the top. From the drop down list, select a phone, any medium that has a portrait orientation, or "responsive" and define a portrait resolution (where the 2nd number is larger than the 1st; tall, not long)

This is the only orientation the website is designed to be compatible for currently. We are working regularly to change that, and your feedback helps!

Simply close this page when you are finished. Your feedback is crucial in shaping our business. Thank you for your time.
