Shipping waste is a serious problem. We're doing our part to keep our carbon footprint low.

As online shopping has become more prevelent, the impact of shipping material waste on the enviroment has been growing exponentially.

Per a report published on Forbes, 165 billion packages are shipped in the United States alone every year. That is the equivilent of 1 BILLION trees in cardboard use. Much of this waste ends up in our oceans, polluting one of the most beautiful places we can go on our planet and devastating sea life and local ecosystems.

That's why have partnered with EcoEnclose. in 2022, EcoEnclose produced over 60 million of these packages out of post-consumer, highly-recyclable materials.

Through creating these recycled packages, EcoEnclose contributed to the savings of 21 million gallons of water, 5412 barrels of oil, 84,580 BTUs of energy, 215,000 trees and saved the expression of 14,448 tons of carbon dioxide into our atmosphere.

We do not want to contribute to the further destruction of the beautiful planet we live on or the ecosystems that we all depend on to survive. Our brand mission is to bring about positive change, and we can not do that in good faith without considering the environemntal implicaitons our actions will have. Altruism and eco-friendliness are two sides of the same coin. We all deserve to be happy and healthy and live on a happy and healthy planet. Please, recycle the containers our products come in. Don't let our efforts be in vain. It will take all of us to effect meaningful change.

Thank you to the excellent team at EcoEnclose for enabling us to offer an eco-friendly shipping solution!
