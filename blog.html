4/6/2023 - "I've spent the majority of the last 2 weeks setting up the groundwork and partnerships we will need to provide you what we have promised. To that end, we have secured an offical partnership with EcoEnclose. We have also spoken to the city of Goodyear, AZ and they have agreed to let us set up a recycling drive at the Goodyear Recreation Campus located at 420 Estrella Parkway on Earth Day, April 22. We will be located by the north entrance, near the soccer pitches. We will have free water and will be giving away tons of merch. We encourage you to come show your support for altruism and social responsibility. All proceeds will be donated to MSF. Merch being given away includes stickers, suckers, laser-engraved pens, recyclable tote bags and cupons for our online store. We are also considering launching a vegan/cruelty-free makeup line, and to that end we have spoken to and requested samples from the same suppliers that work with Alta, Sephora and Free People, among others. We encourage you to use the feedback form on our homepage to suggest items you would like to see added to our store. We need your help to give you what you want most. Our online store catalogue grows larger and more inclusive by the day, so be sure to check back regularly. We will also be adding a 'Past Blogs' page soon, where you'll be able to see all of these updates catergorized by date."
