4/23/2023 - "Great news! Our business has been making strides this week towards our goal of sustainability and giving back to the community. We are excited to share our progress with you all.Firstly, we are thrilled to announce that we have received our new eco-friendly shipping packages from Eco Enclose. The quality of the packages is impressive, and the company's transparency and commitment to the environment have inspired us. We are proud to use these packages to deliver our products to customers and contribute to reducing our carbon footprint.In celebration of Earth Day, we organized our first local event, a recycling drive. Although the turnout was not as big as we had hoped, we were grateful to be able to clean up the park and educate children about our beautiful planet. We are already looking into doing other events, such as charity drives and cute markets to sell our products at.We would like to extend a big thank you to Enroute Coffee and Tea for sponsoring our recycling drive and for promoting us so kindly. We truly appreciate their support and commitment to our cause.Finally, we are still in the process of making and posting more products on our site daily, and we are grateful to everyone who has supported us by making purchases and leaving kind reviews. Your support enables us to continue our journey towards sustainability and giving back to the community.In conclusion, we are proud of the progress we have made this week, and we look forward to continuing to grow and make a positive impact.
